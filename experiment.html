EXPERIMENT

Check formation of DNA-inserted soap bubble using fluorescence

Aim

・Whether the long Scaffold chain can go inside the soap bubble membrane.
・How long can the soap bubble membrane keep its shape with Scaffold chain?

Materials

・Glycerin(和光純薬工業 072-00626):200 μL
・50*TAE(ニッポンジーン 313-90035):20 μL
・TWEEN 20(SIGMA-ALDRICH P9416-50ML):10 μL
・G.W(mQ):770 μL
Mix the four materials described above. Take 97 µL of the mixture and add the following two materials.
・SYBR-Gold:1μL
・b8064:2μL
※Glycerin is use for to keep longer the soap bubble membrane.

Method

We made two types of soap bubbles (①SYBR Gold+b8064 ②SYBR Gold). Exposing them to UV light and observing it. Measure the time until the soap bubbles are broken.
